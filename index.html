About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Giampaolo Bovenzi and I'm an Assistant Professor (RTD-a) at DIETI, University of Napoli Federico II, since October 2023. I received my Ph.D. degree at the same University in June 2022. I'm a member of Traffic research group, headed by the Prof. Antonio Pescapè.
My research interests focus on (anonymized and encrypted) traffic classification, network security (with focus on IoT), and blockchain.
I have co-authored 24 papers in international journals and conference proceedings.
